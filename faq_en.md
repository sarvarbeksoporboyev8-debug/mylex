# YuristAI FAQ
_Last updated: [02.06.2026]_

## 1. What is YuristAI?
YuristAI is an AI legal assistant designed to handle many tasks traditionally done by expensive lawyers. It helps users find, understand, analyze, and work with laws and legal documents published on lex.uz.

## 2. What problem does YuristAI solve?
Legal help is often expensive, slow, and inaccessible. YuristAI removes these barriers by providing fast, affordable, and direct access to Uzbekistan’s laws through AI.

## 3. Does YuristAI aim to replace traditional lawyers?
Yes. YuristAI is built to replace many routine and informational tasks performed by traditional lawyers, especially where speed, cost, and access matter.

## 4. What legal sources does YuristAI use?
YuristAI uses **only official legal documents from lex.uz**. No unofficial sources are used.

## 5. Can I trust the information YuristAI provides?
YuristAI works directly with the official texts published on lex.uz and explains them clearly. Users can always review the original document on lex.uz for full confirmation.

## 6. What can YuristAI help me with?
YuristAI can:
- Explain laws and legal articles
- Summarize legal documents
- Answer questions about rights and obligations
- Help navigate legal procedures
- Clarify complex legal language

## 7. Why is YuristAI better than searching lex.uz manually?
Lex.uz provides official texts, but they are complex and time‑consuming to read. YuristAI instantly explains and interprets them in clear language.

## 8. Can YuristAI make mistakes?
Like any automated system, incorrect outputs are possible. If you believe an answer is wrong, contact us by email so we can review and improve the system.

## 9. How do I report an incorrect answer?
Send an email to:
**yuristai@gmail.com**

Include:
- Your question
- The answer you received
- A reference to the correct lex.uz document (if available)

## 10. Does YuristAI store my questions?
Questions may be stored to improve accuracy, performance, and system quality.

## 11. Does YuristAI work offline?
No. YuristAI requires an internet connection to access lex.uz and generate answers.

## 12. What languages are supported?
YuristAI supports:
- Uzbek (Latin)
- Uzbek (Cyrillic)
- Russian
- English

## 13. Is YuristAI free?
No. YuristAI is a paid service.

## 14. How much does it cost?
Each answer costs **350 UZS**.

## 15. Why is YuristAI paid?
Running AI systems and maintaining accurate legal access has real costs. Pricing is kept low to remain affordable for everyone.
